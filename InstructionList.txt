*Spicy Thai Basil Chicken:
-1 lb Ground chicken.
-2 cups Basil leaves.
-2 Red bell peppers, sliced.
-4 cloves Garlic, minced.
-2-3 Chilies, sliced.
-2 tbsp Soy sauce.
-1 tbsp Fish sauce.
-1 tbsp Sugar.
-2 tbsp Vegetable oil.
-Jasmine rice.
-Instructions:.

*Baked Ziti:
-1 lb Ziti pasta.
-1 lb Ground beef.
-3 cups Marinara sauce.
-1 cup Ricotta cheese.
-2 cups Mozzarella cheese, shredded.
-1 cup Parmesan cheese, grated.
-3 cloves Garlic, minced.
-1 Onion, chopped.
-2 tbsp Olive oil.
-Basil, Oregano, Salt, Pepper to taste.
-Instructions:.
-Cook ziti pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
-In a pan, heat olive oil, add garlic and onion. Sauté until translucent.

-Stir in marinara sauce, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer.
-Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
-In a baking dish, layer cooked pasta, meat sauce, ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan.
-Repeat layers and top with remaining cheese.
-Bake for 25-30 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.

*Creamy Mushroom Risotto:
-1 ½ cups Arborio rice.
-8 oz mushrooms, sliced.
-1 onion, finely chopped.
-2 cloves garlic, minced.
-½ cup white wine.
-4 cups chicken broth.
-½ cup heavy cream.
-2 tablespoons butter.
-½ cup grated Parmesan cheese.
-1 teaspoon thyme.
-Salt and pepper to taste.
-Instructions:.
-In a pan, sauté onions and garlic in butter until translucent.
-Add mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture.
-Stir in Arborio rice and cook for a few minutes until translucent around the edges.
-Pour in white wine, stirring until it's absorbed by the rice.
-Add chicken broth gradually, 1 cup at a time, stirring frequently and allowing the liquid to be absorbed before adding more.
-Once the rice is creamy and cooked but still slightly al dente, stir in heavy cream, Parmesan, thyme, salt, and pepper.
